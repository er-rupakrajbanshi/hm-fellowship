About Us

Himalmedia Fellowship

Himalmedia’s 2023-2024 Fellowship Program is geared towards ensuring better diversity in the newsroom. Through this two-year fellowship, we seek to collaborate with, train and mentor young journalists from backgrounds underrepresented in Nepali media. Our goal is to provide journalists from a wide variety of underrepresented communities with the training and mentoring that they need in order to bring their varied invaluable perspectives to the Nepali reading and viewing public.

We offer entry-level journalists with a competitive salary and we provide a course in journalism and close mentoring by Himalmedia’s senior journalists over the course of two years.

Young journalists from backgrounds underrepresented in terms of ethnicity, caste, geography, and religion are encouraged to apply.

Application Criterias:

  • BA in Mass Communications or related fields (for example, sociology, political science, law,economics, history, literature and languages)

  • Native fluency in Nepali

  • Intermediate level of English

  • Distinction in English in 10+2 or equivalent exam

  • 30 yrs or under

  • Experience in journalism preferred but not required

  • Must be from a community underrepresented in Nepali media in terms of ethnicity, caste, religion, or geography.

Benefits:

  • Learning opportunities through lectures, seminars, and self-study tutorials. These will facilitate problem-based learning with the aim to expand the participants’ depth of knowledge in the field of journalism and the role of a journalist in the modern world

  • Two years of experience working with senior journalists from Himalmedia in Nepali and English-langauge journalism

  • Mentorship by senior journalists from Himalmedia

  • Distinction in English in 10+2 or equivalent exam

  • Possibility of the furthering of employment contract with Himalmedia after two years, pending evaluation

  • A Competitive salary

Responsibilities:

  • Assignments completed as part of the training

  • Three individual features (long-form article + multimedia package) produced over the course of the two-year period

  • Contribution of articles and multimedia content within the first year, and continuing throughout the second year

How to apply?

  • A current CV, including educational background and relevant work experience.

  • A writing sample.

  • A questionaire, and

  • A recent passport-sized photograph
