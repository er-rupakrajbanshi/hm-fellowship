Himalmedia Fellowship Programme

2023-2024 Open call for applications

About Us

Himalmedia fellowship programme 2023-2024 is offering five early-career journalists from underrepresented backgrounds in Nepal the opportunity to train, educate and gather experience in journalism with a team of academic experts in various related subjects and the Himalmedia team. This two-year paid fellowship programme is supported by the International Fund for Public Interest Media.

Fellows have opportunities to research and write stories, contribute to Himalmedia platforms, and create content about current issues in Nepal and the world. And a promising possibility of furthering their career in journalism among professionals in the industry.

Young journalists from backgrounds underrepresented in terms of ethnicity, caste, geography, gender and religion are encouraged to apply. Application closes on the 15th of December 2022.

Eligiblity

  • Bachelors in Mass Communications or related fields : sociology, political science, law, economics, history, literature and languages

  • Native fluency in Nepali

  • Intermediate level of English (Distinction in English in 10+2 or equivalent exam)

  • Age limit: up to 30 years

  • Experience in journalism preferred but not required

  • Underrepresented Nepali Media in terms of ethnicity, caste, geography, gender and religion

Benefits:

  • A Competitive salary

  • Level one basic journalism education via lectures, multimedia materials and self-study tutorials throughout a two year fellowship. The education programme is designed through a problem-based learning approach with the aim to expand the participantsâ€™ depth of knowledge in journalism as well as the role of the journalist in the modern world.

  • Training in content creating, editing applications, publishing, social media platforms and modern journalism in practice

  • Two years of journalist experience working with senior staff at Himalmedia in English and Nepali being part of the news rooms.

  • Mentorship by senior journalists from Himalmedia.

  • Possibility of the furthering of employment contract with Himalmedia after two years, pending evaluation.

Responsibilities:

  • Complete the reading lists and prepare for the lectures.

  • Complete given assignments as a part of the training.

  • Three individual features (long-form article + multimedia package) produced over the course of the two-year period under the supervision of the mentor assigned.

  • Contribution of short articles and multimedia content for Himalmedia platforms throughout the fellowship.

  • A minimum of 85% attendance.

How to Apply ?

Application Submission

  • CV including educational background and relevant work experience

  • A writing sample.

  • Completed questionnaire

  • A recent color photograph

Writing Sample

Please write an essay of no more than 1000 words on one of the following topics:

  • Your view point in current election system of Nepal

  • Human interest story from Nepal current affairs

  • Cultural piece on one of the main festivals of Nepal

OR, provide a writing sample through one of the articles published under your byline or an assignment essay submitted for university as part of your higher studies.
